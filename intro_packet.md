# Welcome to the Coach Josh Boxing Family!

Thank you for joining us at Coach Josh Boxing in Hamden, CT. Whether you are stepping into a boxing gym for the first time or you're an experienced fighter, we are thrilled to have you here. ## 🥊 What to Expect in the Gym
No experience needed. No fitness level required. Coach Josh meets you where you are and walks you through everything step by step. We pride ourselves on a welcoming, judgment-free atmosphere.

*   **Arrive & Connect:** Show up 10 minutes early. We'll introduce you to the community, get you set up, and walk you through the basics.
*   **Warm Up & Technique:** We start with a dynamic warm-up to prevent injury. Then, we break down your stance, footwork, and how to throw a proper punch safely.
*   **Bag Work & Drills:** Zero hard sparring. You'll hit the heavy bags and focus mitts, building power, speed, and real combinations at your own pace.
*   **Conditioning Burnout:** We finish strong with a full-body conditioning circuit. Push-ups, core work, footwork drills — you'll leave feeling accomplished.
